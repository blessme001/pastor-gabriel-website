About Pastor Gabriel

Pastor Gabriel Abimbola Adegboye is a devoted servant of God and the Founder of Worksword of God Church. He is called into ministry with a divine mandate to preach the Word of God, teach righteousness, and provide prophetic insight for individuals and nations.

Through years of dedicated service, Pastor Gabriel has committed himself to spiritual growth, biblical teaching, and national transformation through prayer and the Word of God. His ministry focuses on restoring faith, building strong believers, and guiding people into a deeper relationship with God.

His teachings emphasize holiness, faith, obedience to God, and spiritual discipline. He believes that true national transformation begins with spiritual renewal and a return to God’s principles.

Pastor Gabriel is also a teacher and author who shares divine revelations concerning leadership, faith, and God’s purpose for nations. His ministry continues to impact lives through messages, teachings, and prophetic guidance.
