About Pastor Gabriel

Pastor Gabriel Abimbola Adegboye is a visionary spiritual leader and Founder of Worksword of God Church. Called to preach, teach, and provide prophetic guidance, he has dedicated his life to inspiring individuals and communities toward spiritual growth, righteousness, and national transformation.

Ministry Philosophy

Pastor Gabriel believes that real change — in individuals, families, communities, and nations — begins with spiritual awakening and divine alignment. His ministry blends:

  • Sound Biblical Doctrine
  • Prophetic Insight
  • Practical Teachings for Daily Living
  • National & Social Transformation

Word for the Nation

Pastor Gabriel’s prophetic messages are rooted in God’s guidance for both individuals and the nation. Highlights include:

  • Spiritual Warfare: True transformation requires prayer, fasting, and reliance on God’s power.
  • Call to Righteous Living: Uphold moral integrity and live in the fear of God.
  • National Insight & Guidance: Divine wisdom for leadership, security, and societal growth.

"Many of Nigeria’s challenges require spiritual solutions alongside practical ones. We must seek God’s guidance for lasting peace and prosperity."
