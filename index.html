About Pastor Gabriel

Pastor Gabriel Abimbola Adegboye is a devoted servant of God and the Founder of Worksword of God Church. He is called into ministry to preach the Word of God with clarity and spiritual insight, helping individuals discover their divine purpose and grow in faith.

His spiritual journey began with a deep passion for God and a commitment to understanding the Scriptures. Through prayer, study, and divine encounters, he received a calling into ministry that shaped his life's mission — to guide people toward righteousness and spiritual growth.

Pastor Gabriel’s teachings focus on faith, obedience, righteousness, and divine direction. He believes that God's Word has the power to transform lives and that every believer has a purpose designed by God.

He is also known for his prophetic insight and spiritual guidance. His ministry emphasizes that God still speaks and directs His people today. Through prayer and teaching, he provides encouragement and direction to believers.

His vision includes spiritual revival, national transformation, and raising believers who live according to God’s will.

Worksword of God Church

Worksword of God Church was founded as a place of worship, teaching, and spiritual growth. The church is dedicated to helping believers grow in their relationship with God through sound teaching and prayer.

The ministry focuses on raising individuals who live by faith and demonstrate God's power in their daily lives.

Services include teaching, prayer sessions, prophetic guidance, and spiritual counseling.
