About Pastor Gabriel

Pastor Gabriel Abimbola Adegboye is the Founder of Worksword of God Church, a ministry dedicated to preaching the Word of God with clarity, power, and prophetic insight.

His calling centers on teaching righteousness, guiding believers toward spiritual growth, and delivering messages that inspire transformation in individuals and nations.

Through his teachings and publications, he emphasizes prayer, faith, and obedience as the foundation for a victorious life.

Word for the Nation

Pastor Gabriel shares prophetic insights and spiritual guidance for individuals and the nation.

He teaches that national transformation begins with spiritual awakening and righteous living.
