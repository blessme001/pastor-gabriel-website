About Pastor Gabriel

Pastor Gabriel Abimbola Adegboye is a visionary spiritual leader and Founder of Worksword of God Church. Called to preach, teach, and provide prophetic guidance, he has dedicated his life to inspiring individuals and communities toward spiritual growth, righteousness, and national transformation.

He has ministered to thousands, guiding believers in spiritual development, leadership, and community impact. Pastor Gabriel emphasizes faith, perseverance, and the pursuit of divine purpose in every believer's life. Through his teachings, he encourages ethical leadership, prayerful living, and active community involvement.

With decades of experience in preaching, mentoring, and church leadership, Pastor Gabriel has grown Worksword of God Church into a thriving community of faith, hope, and transformative action.
